About Us.

Our Restaurant Is A Collective Of Amazing People Striving To Build Delightful Indian Food.

Welcome to our family-run Indian restaurant, nestled in the vibrant heart of downtown Bolton. Our passion for authentic, traditional cuisine has inspired us to create a space where the rich flavors and cultural heritage of India come alive. We invite the residents of Bolton and beyond to experience the warmth of our hospitality and the vibrant, diverse tastes of our homeland, lovingly crafted in every dish.

Our food is cooked with traditional methods and passion. The regional specialties are the same that are served at the best tables in India.

These dishes are widely appreciated for their labor-intensive cooking methods and unique flavor arising out of a tried and tested use of herbs and spices.
